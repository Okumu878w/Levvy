About Us

Our History

HM Medical Treatment Solutions was founded in 1995 with a mission to provide cutting-edge medical care to the community. Starting as a small clinic with a handful of dedicated professionals, we have grown into a leading healthcare provider, serving thousands of patients annually.

Our Progress

Over the past three decades, HM has expanded its facilities to include state-of-the-art technology and specialized departments. We’ve introduced innovative treatments, established partnerships with global health organizations, and received numerous awards for excellence in patient care. Today, we are proud to be at the forefront of medical innovation, continuously improving outcomes for our patients.

Our Team

At HM Medical Treatment Solutions, our team is our greatest asset. We currently have 150 doctors and 400 staff members working tirelessly to ensure the best care for our patients. Our professionals are highly trained, with expertise across various medical fields, and are committed to delivering compassionate and personalized care.

We operate across 12 specialized departments, ensuring comprehensive care for all our patients. Our departments include Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, Pediatrics, Oncology, and more.

Our team of doctors includes the following types of specialists:

  • Cardiologists
  • Neurologists
  • Orthopedic Surgeons
  • Pediatricians
  • Oncologists
  • General Surgeons
  • Endocrinologists
  • Dermatologists
